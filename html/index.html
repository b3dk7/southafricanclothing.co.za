about me

My interest in making clothes started around 2015 when i decided that my out of the box edward sizor hands halloween outfit wasnt realistic enough and i needed to make it better. Recently I have aquired a overlocker (also known as a serger) and sewinging machine in order to make and modify clothes at a faster pase. Thinking back, I am no idea why i decided to make girls clothes as seen in the collection above, i guess possibly because a lot of the sewing tutorials I watched on youtube focus on girls clothes. Going forward howerver im motivated to make some boys / unisex clothes.
